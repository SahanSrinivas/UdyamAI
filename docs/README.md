## Elevator Pitch

The first **borrower-facing living Health Card** for Indian MSMEs — a Credit-Karma-style dashboard powered by AA consent flows, ULI's 64-lender rail, and OCEN's Loan Agent standard. MSMEs continuously see their score, know exactly what to fix, and get pre-qualified across banks. Banks get a funnel of self-improved, pre-scored leads.

## Core USP (Kept Anywhere Judges Ask)

*"Every competitor scores you once at application. We score you continuously and tell you how to raise the number before you ever apply."*
